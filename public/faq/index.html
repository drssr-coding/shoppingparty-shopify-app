Frequently Asked Questions

What is Shopping Party?

Shopping Party is a Shopify app that enables real-time collaborative shopping experiences. Customers can shop together, share products, and chat in real-time.

How do I start a shopping session?

Simply click the "Create Shopping Session" button in your dashboard, invite participants via email or link, and start shopping together!

What are the pricing plans?

We offer three plans:

  • Starter ($9.99/month): Basic features for small stores
  • Professional ($29.99/month): Advanced features for growing businesses
  • Enterprise ($99.99/month): Full feature set for large stores

Is my data secure?

Yes! We use industry-standard encryption and security practices to protect your data. All communications are encrypted and we comply with Shopify's security requirements.

How do I get support?

Contact our support team at support@shoppingparty.app or visit our support portal for immediate assistance.
